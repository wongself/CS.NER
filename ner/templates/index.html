CS.NER

CS.NER, a span-based model for named entity recogniton that relies on the pre-trained Transformer network SciBERT as its core. With strong negative sampling, span filtering, and a localized context representation, a search over all spans in an input document becomes feasible. Span-based approaches perform competitive to BILOU-based models and may be the more promising approach for future research due to their ability to identify overlapping entities.

In the future, we plan to investigate more elaborate features of context for entity classifiers.
